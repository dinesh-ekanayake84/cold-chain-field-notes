# Pre-Dispatch Checklist
### Cold-Chain & Temperature-Sensitive Product Logistics

> To be completed by the logistics coordinator before any product leaves the storage facility for a campaign site. No dispatch proceeds without full sign-off.

---

## 1. Product & Batch Verification

- [ ] Product line and batch number confirmed against dispatch order
- [ ] Quantity matches approved dispatch quantity
- [ ] FIFO rotation applied — oldest batch dispatched first
- [ ] Batch number recorded in expiry tracking log

---

## 2. Expiry & Shelf Life Check

- [ ] Expiry date checked for all units in this dispatch
- [ ] Expiry date is sufficient to cover full campaign duration plus buffer
- [ ] No near-expiry units (within threshold window) included unless specifically approved
- [ ] Any flagged units noted and escalated to operations manager before dispatch

---

## 3. Temperature & Cold-Chain Compliance

- [ ] Product temperature verified at point of dispatch
- [ ] Cold storage unit temperature log reviewed — no anomalies in storage period
- [ ] Transport vehicle/cooler unit pre-checked and temperature-compliant
- [ ] Cold-chain continuity plan confirmed for transport route and duration

---

## 4. Packaging & Condition Inspection

- [ ] All units inspected for packaging integrity — no damage, leaks, or compromised seals
- [ ] Damaged or compromised units removed and quarantined
- [ ] Outer packaging/cartons are dry, clean, and intact
- [ ] Any condition issues photographed and logged

---

## 5. Documentation

- [ ] Dispatch order reference number recorded
- [ ] Quantity dispatched entered into expiry tracking log
- [ ] Chain-of-custody documentation completed and signed
- [ ] Receiving campaign site coordinator notified of dispatch details and ETA
- [ ] Driver/delivery team briefed on handling requirements

---

## 6. On-Site Handoff Requirements

- [ ] Receiving site has adequate cold storage or cooling facilities confirmed in advance
- [ ] On-site coordinator briefed on FIFO rotation and expiry dates
- [ ] Post-campaign stock reconciliation requirement communicated to site team
- [ ] Emergency contact for logistics coordinator provided to site team
